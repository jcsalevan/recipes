CHANNA MASALA - Garbanzo Beans in tomato and onion sauce
--------------------------------------------------------

Ingredients:

1         can   Garbanzo Beans
2               Medium sized onions cubed (white) 
3-4             Medium sized roma-tomatoes cubed 
2         tbps  Ginger-garlic paste (can us freshley ground ginger and garlic)
1         tbsp  Garam masala
1/4       tbsp  Turmeric powder
1 & 1/2   tbsp  Corriander powder
2         tbsp  Salt
3/4       tbsp  Spicy Chili powder (paprika powder)
1         tbsp  Cumin seeds
3 + 3     tbsp  Butter
1         tbsp  Sour Cream (or 2 tbsp yogurt)
1 + 1     cup   Water

Method:

Step 1: In a saute pan warm butter, add cumin seeds fry until it slightly darkens. Add cubed onions
	and fry till the rawness is lost for about a min or more on medium heat (not till transperant)
	add the cubed tomatoes and stir fry them for another 30-45 secs just about when the tomatoes 
	start to get watery turn off and dump all the ingredients into a blender with 1 cup water
	and puree it for a min.

Step 2: Warm butter in same pan at meium heat, add the pureed mixture into the pan wash the remaining sause 
	in the blennder with another cup of water and dump it inot the pan. Add garam-masala, salt, chili powder 
	and corriander powder stir them in and lid it. Allow the sause to come to a light boil then add the sour 
	cream you'll see the sause lighten in color, add the can of beans, lid it reduce the hea to low and cook 
	for 8-10 mins. 

Step 3: (Optional) Garnish with 1-2 tbsps chopped cilantro before serving

1 lb semi sweet chocolate
12 eggs
1 lb confecetoners sugar
1 lb butter
2 pints whipping cream
1 tsp vinalla extract
1 TB coffee

melt chocolate and add coffee over double boilder
let eggs sugar and butter come to room temperature
chill cream
beat sugar butter together in stand mixer "creaming" it
add egg yolks one at a time
beat well and add melted chocolate

in a separate bowl beat egg whites and a pinch of salt to form stiff peaks

fold by hand the whites and the chocolate mixture

whip cream and then fold that into the chocolate mixture

freeze entire mixture

remove to fridge 1 hour before serving
